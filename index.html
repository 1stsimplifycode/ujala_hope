Ujjala - A Rising hope for Emerging Society

Our Ujjala platform is a web application we developed to reduce food waste and hunger by transforming surplus food from restaurants and grocery stores into a vital resource for those in need. Similar to food delivery services like Zomato and Swiggy, we enable delivery agencies to transport excess food directly from restaurants to NGOs, which then distribute it to underserved communities. With a user-friendly interface, real-time updates, and automated management of food listings, we ensure timely pickups and only fresh donations. We provide administrators with a comprehensive dashboard to track activities, promoting the vision of "Zero Hunger, Zero Waste" by turning restaurants’ surplus into the poor’s treasure while supporting sustainability in local communities.
